Rupert St John Webster
Director

Rupert has over 12 years' experience in the financial services industry specialising in FX and eFX for the past 10 years. He started his career as a C programmer working for accountancy giant, Sage, before moving to work as a programmer for financial and medical software giant, Misys, on a project with STP and SWIFT.

This experience brought an interest in electronic trading and clearing systems, from STP and clearing models through to pricing, orders, deal capture, position keeping, and eventually hedging, which after a Masters degree in Entrepreneurship, led to an FX operational role with FXall.

Rupert later started Webster Systems and completed his FX knowledge with the ACI Dealing Simulation Course, and working with the BNPP FX desks, and the P2P FX ECN, Kantox.

Rupert was educated at Harrow School, London University, and Bristol University.
