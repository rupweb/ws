ISM Degree

The BSc information systems and management is run by London University at Birkbeck college. The modules covered were:

  • Strategic Management - Industry structure, opportunities and threats, Positioning, Activities structure, Porter.
  • Strategic Information Systems - Systems management, Systems planning, Systems opportunities, Systems projects.
  • Management Accounting - Budgeting, Accounting ratios, Project appraisal, NPV, ROI, Project costing.
  • Financial Accounting � Double entry accounting, Deriving balance sheets, Deriving P&L accounts.
  • Managerial Economics � Supply, demand, elasticity, perfect competition, business cycles.
  • Corporate Finance - Corporate financial strategy, Equity/bond financing, IPO�s, Credit ratings, Bonds pricing.
  • Capital Markets - Swaps Pricing, CAPM, APM, Portfolio theory, Statistical methods, Black/Scholes.
  • Marketing strategy - Market segmentation, Marketing mix, Pricing, Products, Promotions, Distribution.
  • Management research - Qualitative and quantitative research methods, Induction and deduction, Regression.
  • General management � Leadership, Planning, Corporate structure, Theories X, Y and Z.
  • Development Methodologies - RAD, OO, UML, SSADM, YSM, IE, SS, STRADIS.
  • Computer Networking - Transport layers, TCP/IP headers and footers, XML.
  • Programming - Algorithms, Functional design, OO design, Java.
  • Advanced programming - Further OO design, Interfaces and abstract classes, Visual Studio .Net, SQL.
  • Computer Systems - Systems components, systems architecture and binary mathematics, Boolean algebra.
  • IS Concepts - Systems theory, Systems requirements, Systems projects.
  • IS Analysis and Design - SDLC, OO design methods and techniques, Entity diagrams, Logical Data & Data Flow Diagrams.
  • Quantitative methods � Statistical inference, probability and estimation.
  • eCommerce � Commercial opportunities and technologies in E-commerce.
