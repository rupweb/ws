Rupert has over 12 years' experience in the financial services industry specialising in FX and eFX for the past 10 years.

Rupert started his career as a C programmer working for accountancy giant, Sage. He then moved to work for financial and medical software giant, Misys, on a project with SWIFT.

The experience brought an interest in financial pricing, orders, deal capture, position keeping and hedging, which after a Masters degree in Entrepreneurship, led to an FX operational role with FXall, before Rupert started Webster Systems.

His FX knowledge was completed working on the BNPP FX desks, and the P2P ECN, Kantox.

Rupert was educated at Harrow School, London University, and Bristol University.
